# 🚀 Executive Operations Intelligence Dashboard

> **Enterprise Power BI Reporting Solution for Executive Reporting, Operational Analytics, Workforce Intelligence & Business Performance Monitoring**

---

## 📌 Overview

The **Executive Operations Intelligence Dashboard** is a multi-page enterprise Power BI solution designed to provide a centralized 360° view of organizational operations. The solution consolidates strategic initiatives, operational performance, infrastructure monitoring, workforce utilization, organizational KPIs, and employee competency analytics into a single executive reporting platform.

Built using **Power BI**, **Power Query (M)**, and **DAX**, the solution automates data ingestion through a reusable folder-based ETL pipeline, transforms multiple operational datasets into an optimized semantic model, and delivers interactive dashboards that support data-driven decision making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Operational data often exists across multiple spreadsheets and business functions, making it difficult for leadership to obtain a unified view of organizational performance.

This project solves that challenge by consolidating operational data into an enterprise reporting solution that enables executives and managers to:

- Monitor strategic initiatives
- Track operational performance
- Measure workforce utilization
- Monitor organizational KPIs
- Analyze employee skills & certifications
- Support resource planning and executive decision making

---

# 💡 Solution Overview

The reporting solution consists of five integrated dashboards:

- 📊 Executive Operations Dashboard
- 🎯 Strategic Initiatives Dashboard
- 📈 Objectives & Key Results (OKRs) Dashboard
- 👥 Workforce Utilization Dashboard
- 🧩 Skills & Certification Dashboard

Together, these dashboards provide a complete operational intelligence platform for executive reporting.
